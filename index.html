Our Products

Three flagship sealing profiles paired with two specialty solutions—our 3 + 2 lineup spans extrusion, EPDM, silicone and foam assemblies.

Co-extruded profile

Multi-durometer sealing profiles that balance strength and flexibility for HVAC, automotive and industrial junctions.

EPDM rubber profile

Weather- and ozone-resistant EPDM seals for glazing, doors and outdoor enclosures that need long-lasting compression performance.

Rubber Extruded Profile

Custom extruded rubber profiles that deliver consistent cross-sections for piping, cabling and framing applications.

Silicon profile

High-temp silicone gaskets and profiles for ovens, chemical plants and cleanroom assemblies.

Sponge EPDM profile

Closed-cell sponge EPDM profiles that cushion assemblies, reduce vibration and seal against dust.
